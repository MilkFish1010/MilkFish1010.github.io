Computers are made up of two parts: HARDWARE and SOFTWARE.

HARDWARE

Hardware refers to the physical components components of a computer, such as the input devices such as a keyboard, mouse, and monitor. A simple method to identify whether a component of a computer system hardware is designed to be examined: In case you. You can visually perceive it, it's a physical device. NETWORKING

Connecting computers and other devices to share resources such as data and applications is what networking is about.
